Our Story

Art That Speaks
From the Heart

Sim Sketchers was born from a simple belief — that every face tells a story worth preserving. What started as a passion for pencil portraits grew into a full art studio dedicated to capturing Tamil families, elders, children and couples in stunning handmade artwork.

Every sketch, every stroke of paint, is done entirely by hand. No digital filters. No shortcuts. Just raw artistry and genuine love poured into every piece we create.
